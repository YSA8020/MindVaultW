Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy:

  1. Click "Sign Up" on the homepage
  2. Choose your account type (User or Professional)
  3. Fill in your email, password, and basic information
  4. Verify your email address
  5. Complete your profile

What's the difference between User and Professional accounts?

User Accounts: For individuals seeking mental health support. Features include mood tracking, peer support, and access to professional counselors.

Professional Accounts: For licensed mental health professionals and certified peer support specialists. Features include client management, scheduling, and professional resources.

How do I track my mood?

Mood tracking is simple:

  1. Go to your Dashboard
  2. Click "Track Mood" or the mood tracking card
  3. Select your current mood (1-10 scale)
  4. Add optional notes about how you're feeling
  5. Submit your entry

You can track your mood multiple times per day to see patterns and trends.

How do I connect with a counselor?

To connect with a counselor:

  1. Navigate to "Find a Counselor" from your dashboard
  2. Browse available professionals or use filters
  3. View counselor profiles and specializations
  4. Send a connection request
  5. Schedule your first session

All counselors are verified, licensed professionals.

Account Settings

How do I change my password?

To change your password:

  1. Go to Settings → Security
  2. Click "Change Password"
  3. Enter your current password
  4. Enter your new password (minimum 8 characters)
  5. Confirm your new password
  6. Click "Update Password"

How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile:

  1. Go to Settings → Profile
  2. Click "Edit Profile"
  3. Update any information you'd like to change
  4. Click "Save Changes"

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account:

  1. Go to Settings → Account
  2. Scroll to "Delete Account"
  3. Click "Delete My Account"
  4. Confirm your decision
  5. Enter your password to confirm

Warning: This action cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted.

Troubleshooting

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password?"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Click the link and create a new password

The page is not loading. What should I do?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
  2. Clear your browser cache
  3. Try a different browser
  4. Disable browser extensions temporarily
  5. Check your internet connection
  6. Contact support if the issue persists

I'm not receiving email notifications. What's wrong?

If you're not receiving emails:

  1. Check your spam/junk folder
  2. Verify your email address in Settings
  3. Check your email notification preferences
  4. Make sure noreply@mindvault.fit is whitelisted
  5. Contact support if the issue continues

How do I report a bug or issue?

To report a bug or issue:

  1. Go to Settings → Help & Support
  2. Click "Report an Issue"
  3. Describe the problem in detail
  4. Include screenshots if possible
  5. Submit your report

You can also email us at support@mindvault.fit
