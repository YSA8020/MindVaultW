Calming Breathing Exercise

When you're feeling overwhelmed, try this simple breathing exercise to help calm your mind and body.

Breathe In

Follow the circle: Breathe in as it expands, breathe out as it contracts.

Repeat for 5-10 cycles to feel calmer.
